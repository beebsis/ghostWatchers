starter guide

written by WOL14M

Ghost Watchers is totally about haunting evil spirits.

    The process of haunt consists of 3 stages:
  • Determine the special properties of the ghost
  • Perform the right weakening ritual
  • Catch a ghost using special trap

Special ghost properties: Type,
Type, Age and Mood. Each property can be determined using special tools

To collect evidence and read hints, use TAB. You will see a pad, in the left part of which you need to indicate the collected evidence. In the right part you need to select the correct properties of the ghost. The selected evidence from the left part will help to select the propreties in the right part. There are two buttons at the bottom of the pad: Encyclopedia. This section contains descriptions of all the ghosts and tools Weakening the ghost.

This section provides a list of actions to weaken the ghost. In the right part there is a hint on the action of the tools on the selected type of ghost Each correct action to weaken the ghost will be accompanied by the sound of a gong and a hint (at an normal difficulty level). If you perform actions in the correct sequence, but do not see these effects - most likely, you made a mistake with the properties of the ghost. Check it again! There are also some additional quests that gives you coins. Coins can be immediately spent on the purchase of tools. Purchased tools are not transferred to the next session.

Difficulties

We recommend you to start from the normal difficulty. You may see hints on this difficulty.

Hard Difficulty:

You lose your tools when the ghost takes you away The ghost can lock the front door at the moment of attack

Insane Difficulty:

The ghost can break electric tools in your hands Ghost can attack hunters outside the house Many items have got only one charge

Item information and description

Motion Sensor

A device that helps to determine the location of the ghost. Place the device on the floor, and when a ghost approaches the device directs a laser beam in it's direction.

Plasma Absorber

A device that can weaken a ghost. If you made a few mistakes when catching a ghost and seriously angered him, then just use a plasma absorber, it is able to drive away the spirit for a while.

This device has a fairly long radius and it's recommended to place around the center of the room. Everyone should still be using other handheld protectives meanwhile within it's range for a higher chance of surviving a hunt.

Danger detector

This is a gadget that will allow you to stay at a safe distance from the ghost. Upon close contact with the spirit the devices begins to beep loudly, which helps you escape from the threat.

Withering Light

An incredibly strong source of light that can scare away a ghost for a long enough peroid of time, but be careful while using it, do not blind your teammates.

Holy salt

Holy salt calms the ghost, you need to be very accurate and fairly close in order to throw any salt and have a high chance of success.

Fire salt

Fire salt can stun for a few seconds works as regular salt on strong ghosts, you need to be very accurate and fairly close in order to throw any salt and have a high chance of success.

salt

Drives away the ghost for a short period of time.

Video Camera with motion sensor

This Video Camera will turn on once it has detected motion in it's area and you'll be able to use it to cover a bigger area.

Photo Camera with motion sensor

Similar to the Motion sensor device, that will turn itself in the range of the ghost and take a photo of it.

Oija board

The Oija board can give you different answers, but the way to use it is very simple. Simply put it down near where you think the ghost is, then after a fair amount of activity it can start to move from side to side, randomly or not move at all.

EMF Sensor

The device is used to detect ghost activity - each ghost is different in terms of EMF, you have the 5 EMF levels and then you got the extra 3 RED. Based on these you'll be able to figure out more about the ghost.

UV Flashlight

This is used to find footsteps and help you identify the ghost. There's currently no fingerprints other similar in the game.

Video & camera tripod

Simply just a running video camera on a tripod and a photo camera on a tripod.

Voodoo Doll

So, the Voodoo Doll is something that helps us identify the ghost. The Voodoo Doll is great together with the radio for communication.

Particle Counter

The particle counter will count the air particles around it, the higher the older the ghost. If you got 100-500 it's old, if you got 500-1000 it's ancient etc.

Thermometer

Thermometer displays the temps of where you're looking at, this is a very great tool early game to figure out where the ghost is or where it's regularly walking. If the temp skyrockets, you'll know it's a demon.

Flaslights, lighter & lightning systems

Small flashlight is a normal flashlight, the bigger one is a more powerful one. The lighter you can use to light candles with, that's about it currently.

The lightning system is simply just a big major stationary light source that's great for lightning up a room all the time.

Photo Camera & Video Camera

These are mainly used by objective task. The video Camera can show you if the Ghost is close since it'll get distorted if it is.

Holy Water, Golden Bomb & Silver Bomb

Holy water can get rid of the ghost for you.

Gold bomb and Silver bomb both acts as flashes but with different dust. Used to drive away many ghosts, but ghosts can be immune to them.

Christ Statue, Incense, Cross

Theese items will save you from getting killed in a hunt. However, remember to KEEP this in your hand before you get attacked or you will die.

Holy Fire

Mostly used in weaknings, not much other info about it currently.

Ritual Dagger

This item will make the ghost angry at everyone and become visible for a peroid of time as well - great way to anger the dead.

Magic Powder

The Magic Powder will help you see where the ghost is walking upon contact with it. It'll leave a faint glowing trail as it moves for a period of time..

Ghost Catcher

The Ghost Catcher is the final equipment you'll be using in this game, after you've weakened the ghost in the correct order - you'll need to right click throw this item at the ghost, if it doesn't work then you most likely failed at one of the weakning points and will have to figure out which one and do it again..

Radio

Can be used to ask questions and possibly get a response - remember to listen carefully.

How to catch a ghost

You need to complete all the tasks from the weakening list in the correct order.

If you have done 3 tasks correctly, but the 4th one is wrong, you do not need to start from the beginning.

At a normal level of difficulty, you can understand that the correct action has been done by the message that appears at the top of the screen and the special sound.

If you have done all the actions correctly, then the ghost will walk around the house in a visible state. Throw the catcher at the ghost.
